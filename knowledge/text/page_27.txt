The referee does not have to wait for a player to have possession of the ball to
start the five-second countdown. The countdown can also be started if the
player deliberately causes a delay by:
• slowly retrieving the ball
• taking up an incorrect position at a throw-in
• putting the ball in an incorrect position at a goal kick
As the purpose of the countdown is to maintain the tempo of the match by
preventing time-wasting at throw-ins and goal kicks, the player should not be
penalised if they are in the process of throwing/kicking the ball as the referee
reaches the end of the countdown.
