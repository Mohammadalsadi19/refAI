237 The shot by a team-mate (A) rebounds off or is deflected by an opponent to attacker (B), who is penalised for playing or touching the ball having previously been in an offside position. An attacker (C) is in an offside position, not interfering with an opponent, when a team-mate (A) passes the ball to player (B1) in an onside position who runs towards the opponents’ goal and passes the ball (B2) to team-mate (C). Attacker (C) was in an onside position when the ball was passed, so cannot be penalised.
